Tournament Format

The tournament will consist of nine, 80 minute hanchan in a round robin format with no cutoff. Tables that exceed 80 minutes will finish their current hand and play a final hand, if applicable, then proceed to record their scores and summon a referee to check and sign the scoresheet before leaving the table.

WRC 2025 Rules and Standards of Conduct

ARMO 2025 will utilise the standard 2025 WRC ruleset.

The WRC ruleset is the first international ruleset to be certified for competition play by authorities in Japan, Europe and the Americas. Since its introduction at the first World Riichi Championship in 2014, it has been updated and improved year over year to be a definitive guide to riichi mahjong play.

All players are required to adhere the WRC Standards of Conduct at all times throughout the event. Failure to abide by these standards of conduct may be subject to penalty (including disqualification without refund and immediate expulsion from the venue) at the sole discretion of the tournament organisers. We are committed to building a safe and welcoming environment for all, and we operate a zero tolerance policy towards abuse, intimidation, or harassment of players or staff. All players are also strongly encouraged to follow the gameplay guidelines during play.

These guidelines have been devised to ensure that games proceed smoothly, and to protect the integrity of the game by preventing opportunities for cheating, collusion, or common mistakes.

Transport Options

Most public transport systems (not including Airtrain) in Brisbane and the surrounding area have a flate rate of 50c per trip. You can pay for fares with your phone, debit/credit card, paper tickets or by using a Go card which can be purchased at any newsagency. It is recommdended to use a navigation service like Google Maps to provide accurate directions.

-- If travelling by train: You can take any train line to Central Station with a 12 minute walk to the venue.

-- If travelling by bus: When travelling from outside the CBD, take a bus to King George Square then a 7 min walk to the venue. It is generally faster to walk if you are already in the CBD.

-- If travelling by CityCat (ferry): Disembark from North Quay terminal then walk 15 minutes to the venue.

-- If travelling by car: There is limited paid street and parking complex parking which can be found on Mary Street and surrounding streets.

Venue

Brisbane, the capital of Queensland --the Sunshine State-- is a relaxed city that blends a growing cultural scene with plenty of outdoor charm. Set along the Brisbane River, it offers a mix of modern city life and natural beauty, with highlights like South Bank’s riverside parks, galleries, and casual dining spots. With its warm weather, easygoing pace, and access to beaches and nature, Brisbane is a comfortable and welcoming place to explore.

ARMO 2025 will be held at Four Points by Sheraton Hotel located in the heart of the Brisbane CBD. With easy access to all the top attractions including Queen's Wharf, South Bank, QPAC, Riverstage, Brisbane Botanic Gardens, and Queen St Mall you will find both comfort and convenience in abundance at your fingertips.
